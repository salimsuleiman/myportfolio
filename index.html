More about me

Salim Suleiman Abubakar is my name, and I was born and raised in Nigeria. I am currently a student at this university with a computer science major. When I was a child, I was somewhat of an introvert and a curious person who was always nagging for technology and loved messing with my mom's phone so that I could fix my parents' phone settings. When I finally got my first computer, I was simply playing around with it. When I was eventually introduced to programming, I spent the entire night watching YouTube videos and reading online articles about it. Before I knew it, I was hooked I am self-taught when it comes to technology, as I previously stated. I spend much of my time learning things online because there isn't any chance to learn them physically in the first place.

Growing up without anyone to discuss my interests with has been my biggest challenge in my career as a coder. Yes, I have family, but even though they are encouraging, they do not recognize the significance of what I am learning and doing or show any adoration for it to keep me motivated. Therefore, even though I build projects, I'm used to simply enjoying and appreciating my own work, so I must be my own inspiration and motivation. To keep going like this without getting discouraged takes a lot of passion.

My dream is to become a successful software engineer, either in the sense of starting my own business one day or simply working for a company that will have a positive impact on humanity. If I am successful in my current freelancing work, I hope to work abroad for a company. I made this promise to myself in the hopes that one day I will be able to reflect on the past and be proud of my accomplishments.
